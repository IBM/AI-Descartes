----------------------------------------------------
I.16.6
----------------------------------------------------
-------------
Formula
-------------
v_x = (u+v_x')/ (1+(u*v_x'/c^2))
-------------
BG-Theory
-------------
(1) v_x	= x / t
(2) x'	= v_x' * t'
(3) x 	= (x' + u* t') /(sqrt(1-(u^2/c^2)))
(4) t 	= t' +(u*x'/c^2)/(sqrt(1-(u^2/c^2)))
-------------
Proof
-------------
substitute (2) for x' in (4) obtaining (5)
substitute (5) for t and (3) for x in (1)
